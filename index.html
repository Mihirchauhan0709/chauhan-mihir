About Me

Hey, I'm Mihir Chauhan; a Data Science grad student at CU Boulder who turns caffeine and curiosity into AI systems that actually work.

From building CloudSense (an AI code review platform) to an AI Interview Coach that's probably better at interviews than I am. I love shipping things end to end. Most recently, I've been doing research at CU Anschutz, working on clinical NLP and getting LLMs to generate medical infographics (because doctors deserve nice visuals too).

When I'm not wrangling ML pipelines or deploying stuff to the cloud, you'll find me on the football pitch pretending I'm prime Messi, hiking Colorado trails, or vibe coding at 2 AM with lo-fi beats on. My superpower? Taking a messy, complex idea and turning it into a clean, scalable system and actually finishing it.
